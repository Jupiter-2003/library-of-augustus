Book Of The Month
God Emperor of Dune
written by Frank Herbert
Leto Atreides, son of the prophet Paul Muad'Dib, is the virtually immortal God Emperor of Dune. He alone understands the future, and he knows with a terrible certainty that the evolution of his race is at an end unless he can breed new qualities into his species. But to achieve his final victory, Leto Atreides must also bring about his own downfall...
